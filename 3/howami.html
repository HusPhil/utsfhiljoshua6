HOW AM I?

A few days ago, my answer to this question would have been the cliche, "I'm okay." But after thinking deeply about "where I am" now, I am uncertain of how well I am really doing today.

I am anxious. This new environment called college is very different compared to junior and senior high schools in the past. Not only was I forced to move out of my house, but college also comes with a contract that basically robs you of the time to relax and enjoy being alive.

I am lonely. Every time I have to say goodbye to my home, sorrow instantly plagues my mind. I couldn't think of wise words that could ease the sadness; the only option is a sigh of compromise.

Nevertheless, the world will keep on spinning whether I laugh or cry. The past is full of nostalgic yet regretful experiences, and the future makes me depressed out of anxiety. So, by elimination, the present is likely the happiest time.
