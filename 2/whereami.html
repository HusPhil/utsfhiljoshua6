WHERE AM I?

Reminiscing is certainly wistful; I suddenly remembered the days so long ago, when I foolishly wished I could grow up faster. "Folish indeed," I sigh, because now I constantly fight the desire to go back in time and rewind.

Where I am right now is the place I have reached after countless battles. Going through these battles developed me as a person. They enabled me to learn new things and discover more about the world we live in, as well as broaden my knowledge of some of my strengths and weaknesses.

Still, the journey must continue, and the place I am right now has even more difficult battles waiting to be conquered. Although I am anxious I might fail them, I still won't give up without a fight. Knowing your fears and limitations can be disheartening, but I believe it can still help you prepare for battle nonetheless.
